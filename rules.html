Family Points Rules

Daily Routine

  • Get dressed in the morning
  • Make your bed
  • Bring out your plate after meals
  • Brush your teeth after breakfast and dinner
  • Shower every other day and tidy up your bathrobe
  • Charge your iPad and tidy up folders for the next day
  • Put on your pajamas
  • Put dirty laundry in the hamper
  • Lay out clothes for the next day
  • Go to bed on time (preferably read a bit, always by 8:30 pm, lights out at 9 pm)
  • At the table: no singing, dancing, playing with hands, tying hair, eating with hands if possible, or putting feet on chairs
  • Don't act like a parent
  • Don't interrupt while others are talking
  • Don't make mom repeat things
Points: 15 points are earned each day.
Penalties: -1 point for each rule broken.
Weekly Limit: Monday to Friday, max. 75 points or 5 francs (1 franc per day, each point is worth ~0.07 francs).
Important: The 5 francs are only awarded with 75 points. If any points are lost, they must be recovered with rewarded behaviors.

Rewarded Behaviors

  • Set the table for everyone (+5 points)
  • Hang out the washing (+5 points)
  • Take out Emy (+15 points)
  • Take out the garbage (+5 points)
  • Clean the rabbit area (+15 points)
  • Order drawers or closets in the bedroom/playroom (+15 points)
  • General cleaning (+15 points)

School

  • Minimum grade 4.5 (for Linda: 5). (-75 points if grade is lower)
  • General monthly average ≥ 5 (Linda: 5.4). (+75 points or 5 francs)
  • Average ≥ 5 in all subjects at month end. (+150 points or 10 francs)
